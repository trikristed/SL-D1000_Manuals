# ASF Encoder Cover

  1. Pull the lever (A) and open the ASF Cover Unit (B).
  2. Remove the single screw, and then remove the ASF Cover Band (B) from the ASF Cover Unit (A).
    • : Silver/M3x8/Cup S-tite screw
  3. Remove each single screw and then remove the 2 Shaft Support (A), and then remove the ASF Cover Unit (B).
    • : Silver/M3x12/Bind S-tite screw
  4. Pull the tabs to remove the Paper Feed Unit (A).
  5. Remove the 2 screws, and then lift up and remove the ASF Vertical Feed Unit (A).

    • :  Silver/M4x10/Bind S-tite screw
    Assembly / 組み立て

    Fix the 2 hooks (B) of the ASF Vertical Feed Unit on the printer to install it.
  6. Remove the 2 screws securing the ASF Main Unit (A).

    • : Black/M4x12/Bind S-tite screw
  7. Remove the 2 screws securing the ASF Main Unit (A).

    • : Black/M4x12/Bind S-tite screw

  8. Lift up the printer (B) from the ASF Main Unit (A) and remove it.


    Lubrication / 注油

    Before attaching the part, refer to the following and then lubricate.

    • Lubrication
  9. Remove the 5 screws securing the ASF Left Cover Assy (A).
    • : Silver/M3x10/Bind P-tite screw
    • : Black/M3x10/Bind P-tite screw
  10. Remove the single screw, and then remove the ASF Left Cover Assy (A).
    • : Silver/M3x10/Bind P-tite screw
  11. Remove the single screw and remove the ASF Encoder (A).
    • : Silver/M2x6/Bind P-tite screw

  12. Remove the cable (A) from the connector and then remove the ASF Encoder (B).

    Assembly / 組み立て
    • When installing, align the dowels (A) of the ASF Encoder with the positioning holes (B).

    • When installing, fit the ASF Motor Scale (B) in the slit (A) of the ASF Encoder.
  13. Remove the single screw, and then remove the ASF Encoder Cover (A).
    • : Silver/M2x6/Bind P-tite screw
